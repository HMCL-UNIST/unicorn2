This guide walks you through the VESC Tool download process, from account creation to the download email.

## Create a VESC Project account

To download VESC Tool, you need to sign up on the VESC Project site first.

- [https://vesc-project.com/vesc_tool](https://vesc-project.com/vesc_tool)

## Free version “purchase” flow

Even though VESC Tool has a free version, the download process looks like a checkout flow. Follow these steps.

![VESC Tool page](/assets/img/posts/vesc-tool-download/image.png)

1. Click `Add to cart`.

![Cart view](/assets/img/posts/vesc-tool-download/image-1.png)

2. Click `Checkout`.

![Checkout view](/assets/img/posts/vesc-tool-download/image-2.png)

3. Click `Review order` without entering extra information.

![Order review view](/assets/img/posts/vesc-tool-download/image-3.png)

The free version is sufficient for most basic use cases.

## Download from the email

Click `Submit order` and a download email will be sent to you.

![Download email example](/assets/img/posts/vesc-tool-download/image-4.png)

Open the email and choose the file that matches your operating system.

## Wrap-up

Remember that VESC Tool is free, but the download flow is formatted like a purchase. If you follow `Add to cart → Checkout → Review order → Submit order`, the download link will arrive by email. Select the file for your operating system and install it.
