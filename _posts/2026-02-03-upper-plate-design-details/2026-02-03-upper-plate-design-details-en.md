## Notes before applying the upper plate

This upper plate design applies to **Serpent SRX8 GTE '23 1/8 4WD EP** and **Traxxas 740544 Ford Fiesta ST Rally 1/10**.

The design was created in Solidworks, and both **SLDPRT** and **STL** files are shared.

To minimize sensor uncertainty due to flex, the plates are designed at **5T–5.5T**. Stiffness depends on infill and material, so adjust as needed for your environment. (Infill 20%, triangle pattern, ABS reference)

There is no dedicated space for mounting a BEC. We fixed it to the underside of the upper plate with **double‑sided silicone tape**.
